    About Me

My attention to detail and experience working as part of a team are a good fit for web development. I strive to write clean, understandable code, and am always seeking to improve my skills and learn new technologies.

    I live in the Pittsburgh area with my wife and our Siberian Husky. I enjoy sci-fi movies, astronomy, hiking, and traveling with my wife.
