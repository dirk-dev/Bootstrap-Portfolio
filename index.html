About Me

I attended Indiana University of Pennsylvania, where I earned a B.A. in Journalism. I have worked as a prepress technician in the commercial printing industry for over 20 years, and have gained much experience working with design apps such as Adobe InDesign,Illustrator, and Photoshop, as well as specialized software such as PDF editors,RIPs, and press sheet layout software.

I have been wanting to get into computer programming for some time, and am excited about the opportunity for me to become a web developer.

I live in the Pittsburgh area with my wonderful wife and our rambunctious Siberian Husky. I enjoy sci-fi movies, astronomy, hiking, and traveling with my wife.
